Victoria Sung

Project 2: Liminal Spaces

This project explores the combination of various materials and techniques to represent dreams and hopes. The pieces incorporate elements such as paint and oil pastel to convey different themes and emotions. I experiment with layering and texture to create depth and movement within the compositions.Below are some examples from the series.

Medium: acrylic paint, oil pastel

Year: 2025

Size: 18 x 24 inches

Artist Statement

My body of work combines mixed media and poetry to explore themes of loss, tragedy, and hope. Much of my work is influenced by growing up in suburban Ohio to Korean immigrant parents.
