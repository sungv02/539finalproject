Project 1: Bedside Mourning

This project explores tragedy amidst rising medical costs and uncertainty.

Artist Statement

My body of work combines mixed media and poetry to explore themes of loss, tragedy, and hope. Much of my work is influenced by growing up in suburban Ohio to Korean immigrant parents. I am interested in capturing how wider economic and cultural forces affect the daily lives of individuals.
