Victoria Sung

Project 1: Bedside Mourning

This project explores tragedy amidst rising medical costs and uncertainty. The illustrations were created using oil pastels to convey a sense of loss and vulnerability. The first piece in the series captures my aunt in a hospital bed while receiving kidney dialysis treatment. Her soul looms over her bedside, caught between life and death. The second piece depicts her three children and their grief as they may lose their mother.

Medium: oil pastel

Year: 2024

Size: 6 x 8 inches

Artist Statement

My body of work combines mixed media and poetry to explore themes of loss, tragedy, and hope. Much of my work is influenced by growing up in suburban Ohio to Korean immigrant parents.
